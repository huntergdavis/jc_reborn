Johnny sits down to read a book under his tree.
A mermaid swims up to the island.
Johnny throws away his book and runs to the mermaid.
Johnny seems nervous, and acts shy.
The mermaid gives Johnny a gift, a necklace.
Johnny wonders what he can give back to the mermaid.
He has an idea, and throws the mermaid a life preserver.
Johnny proposes a date to the mermaid, who gives him the green light.
The mermaid swims away, and Johnny waves happily
