Johnny walks to the edge of the island.
He builds a sand castle.
A 16th century three-mast ship sails into view.
Tiny lilliputians row ashore as Johnny climbs the palm tree.
The lilliputians climb into the castle, and claim it as their own.
Tiny airplanes fly out of the castle,  and begin to attack Johnny atop the tree.
Johnny swats at the tiny planes atop the palm tree  in a scene akin to King Kong.
Johnny falls into the ocean comically.
